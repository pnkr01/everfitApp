Everfit Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Everfit ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

---

### 1. Introduction

Everfit is an AI-powered health and fitness tracking application designed to help you achieve your wellness goals. By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

---

### 2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide, maintain, and improve our services.

#### 2.1 Personal Information Provided by You

When you create an account or use the App features, you provide us with:

* **Identity Data:**
    * **Email Address:** Collected via Google Sign-In for account creation, authentication, and recovery.
    * **Name & Photo URL:** Collected via Google Sign-In for personalization and profile display.
* **Health and Anthropometric Data:**
    * **Age, Gender, Height, Weight:** Used to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).
    * **Activity Level and Goals (Lose/Maintain/Gain):** Used to determine your personalized calorie and macro targets.
    * **Food Logs & Water Intake:** User-entered data on daily consumption, which forms the core of your tracking history.

#### 2.2 Automatically Collected Data

When you use the App, we automatically collect:

* **Usage Data:** Information about how you use the App (e.g., screens visited, features accessed, time spent).
* **Device Data:** Device type, operating system version (iOS/Android), and unique device identifiers (via Firebase App Check for security).
* **Location Data:** General location derived from your IP address or timezone setting, necessary for accurate scheduled notifications.

---

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Data Used | Rationale |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Personalized Goal Setting** | Health & Anthropometric Data | To provide accurate, calculated calorie, protein, carbohydrate, and fat targets. |
| **Core Service Functionality** | Food Logs, Water Intake | To display daily progress, charts, and history on the Dashboard. |
| **Account Management** | Identity Data | To secure your account, process sign-in, and manage your user profile. |
| **Notifications & Reminders** | Usage Data, Timezone | To send scheduled push notifications for meals and hydration (if enabled). |
| **Security & Integrity** | Device Data | To prevent unauthorized access and protect the App from abuse (via Firebase App Check). |

---

### 4. Data Storage and Security

* **Storage Location:** Your data is encrypted and stored on **Google Cloud Firestore** servers.
* **Security Measures:** We utilize industry-standard security practices, including:
    * **Encryption at Rest:** Your data is encrypted on the server side using AES-256 encryption.
    * **Encryption in Transit:** All communication between your device and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption.
    * **Access Control:** Access to production data is strictly limited to authorized personnel necessary for maintenance and support.

---

### 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not sell, rent, or trade your Personal Information to any third parties or advertisers.**

---

### 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you have the following rights:

* **Access and Correction:** You can view, modify, or correct your anthropometric data and food logs at any time within the App settings.
* **Opt-Out of Notifications:** You can disable push notifications for meals and hydration at any time via the **Settings** screen in the App.
* **Account Deletion:** You have the right to request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data. This can be performed directly within the App: **Settings** > **Account Zone** > **Delete Account**. This action is irreversible and immediately scrubs all data from our servers.

---

### 7. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for users who are at least 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect Personal Information from children under 13. If you believe we may have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

---

### 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, notifying you within the App or via email.
